Access timetable & fee structure using school email through Microsoft outlook

As a student, staying organized and informed is crucial for academic success. One tool that can greatly assist in this endeavor is your school email, typically provided through Outlook. By signing into Outlook, you gain access to a wealth of valuable resources, including timetables, fee structures, and important communications from Pioneer International University administration.


In this blog post, we will walk you through the steps to log into Outlook and harness its power to streamline your academic journey.


What you need to do:

1. Open your web browser: Launch your preferred web browser (e.g., Chrome, Firefox, Safari) on your computer or mobile device or download Microsoft outlook from playstore.


2. Visit the Outlook login page: In the address bar, type "outlook.com" or "login.microsoftonline.com" and press Enter. This will take you to the Outlook login page.


3. Enter your school email address: On the Outlook login page, locate the field labeled "Email, phone, or Skype" and enter your school-provided email address. It typically follows the format"name.surname@students.piu.ac.ke"


4. Click "Next": After entering your email address, click the "Next" button to proceed.


5. Enter your password: On the next screen, you will be prompted to enter your password. Type in your school email account password carefully.


6. Click "Sign in": Once you have entered your password, click the "Sign in" button to log into your Outlook account.


7. Navigate to your inbox: After successfully signing in, you will be redirected to your Outlook mailbox. This is where you can find all your emails, including those sent by your school.


8. Access timetables: Look for emails with attachments or links related to your timetable. Often, schools send out schedules at the beginning of the academic year or semester. Click on the relevant email and open the attachment or follow the link to access your timetable.


9. Explore fee structures: Schools often share fee structures, payment deadlines, and related information through email. Look for emails from the school's administration or finance department. Open the email and review the content to understand the fee structure and any other pertinent details.


10. Stay informed with school communications: Outlook is an excellent platform for schools to communicate important announcements, updates, and events. Regularly check your inbox for emails from your teachers, school administrators, or various school departments to ensure you don't miss any crucial information.


Make it a habit to check your school email regularly to stay organized and well-informed throughout your academic journey.
