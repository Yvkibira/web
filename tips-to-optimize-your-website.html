Discover the Best Ways to Improve Your Website's Performance



so you have just finished making your website here are some useful tips to make use of :

Convert your images to .webp format to make your website load faster. Png and jpeg photos take up a lot of space, which can slow down your website. By using a free online tool like Convertio, you can quickly convert your images to .webp format, which is optimized for web use and loads much faster.

Track website visits and get daily insights with Google Analytics. By adding Google Analytics tags to your website, you can track user behavior, measure engagement, and get insights into what's working and what's not. This will help you make data-driven decisions to improve your website's performance and user experience.

Make your website discoverable by submitting a sitemap to Google Search Console. Sitemaps provide a roadmap of your website's structure and hierarchy, which helps search engines crawl and index your content more effectively. Use a free sitemap generator like XML Sitemap Generator to create your sitemap, then upload it to your website's hosting server and submit it to Google Search Console. This will help your website appear in search results and increase its visibility.

Create a professional-looking logo for your website using Small SEO Tools Designer. A logo is an essential part of your website's branding and can help you stand out from the competition. With Small SEO Tools Designer, you can easily create custom logos and posters that fit your website's style and tone.

Measure your website's SEO performance with Seobility. Seobility is a free online tool that analyzes your website's content, structure, and technical elements to give you actionable insights into how to improve your SEO. By using Seobility regularly, you can avoid common SEO mistakes and optimize your website for better rankings and visibility.

By using these tools, you can optimize your website, improve its performance, and increase its visibility in search results. So what are you waiting for? Start using these tools today and take your website to the next level!
