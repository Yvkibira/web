How to access and download Pioneer International University past papers

Preparing for an exam can be an overwhelming task, but one thing that can help you immensely is practicing with past papers. By doing so, you will get familiar with the exam format, types of questions asked, and improve your time management skills.

In this blog, we will take you through the steps on how to download past exam papers from the Pioneer international university website.

Step 1: Go to https://piu.ac.ke

Step 2: Scroll down to University Library On the homepage, scroll down until you see the "University Library" section.

Step 3: Click on the "University Library" and it will redirect you to the library's homepage.

Step 4: Then scroll down and tap Past Papers Once on the library's homepage, Click on it, and it will take you to a new page.

Step 5:You will be taken to Pioneer international university past papers repository section

The past papers page will direct you to the university's repository page, where you will find all the past exam papers sorted by the year of the exam.

Step 6: Tap which school you are in Select the school that you are in from the list of schools on the page.

Step 7: Then choose which unit you want ,the download will automatically start Select the unit that you want to download past papers for from the list of units available for that school. Click on the unit's name, and it will automatically start downloading.
