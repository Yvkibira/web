Side Hustles for students in kenya

As a student, balancing academics, social life, and financial responsibilities can be challenging. One has to get creative and explore some ways to make money. With the technologies advancing there has never been a better time for students to explore side hustles that offer flexibility, convenience, and extra income. In this article, we will explore some captivating side hustle opportunities tailored specifically for students.


1. Delivery Jobs with Glovo, Uber Eats, and Jumia:

In recent years, on-demand delivery services like Glovo, Uber Eats, and Jumia have revolutionized the way people order food and other essentials. Students can leverage their free time, bicycles, or motorcycles to join these platforms as delivery partners. With the flexibility to choose their own working hours, students can earn money while zipping around the city, delivering meals and groceries to customers.


2. Blog Guest Writing:


If you have a knack for writing or you can gather data and make it useful, guest blogging can be a rewarding side hustle. Numerous websites, such as Kenya Yote, accept guest articles on various topics. By sharing your expertise or experiences through well-crafted blog posts, You can earn recognition, expand their network, and even generate income through sponsored content or affiliate marketing.


3. Affiliate Marketing:


Affiliate marketing is a popular side hustle for students looking to monetize their online presence.Gone are the days you go online and feel regret because you can do something like promoting products or services of companies like Jumia, Glovo, Safaricom and global companies like , amazon ,apple ,SEOability and more.Students can earn commissions for every successful referral. This side hustle requires minimal investment and can be done from the comfort of your own home, making it an ideal option for students.


4. Reselling Products Using Jiji Platform:


The rise of online marketplaces like Jiji has opened up a world of possibilities for aspiring entrepreneurs. You can utilize this platform to buy products at discounted prices from places like China Square and resell them at a profit. Whether it's trendy fashion items, electronics, or household goods, Jiji provides a convenient platform to connect with buyers and create a successful reselling business.


5. Review Websites and Give Recommendations:


Students with a passion for sharing their opinions and experiences can turn their knack for reviewing into a profitable side hustle. Many websites and platforms pay individuals to write honest reviews of products, services, or even travel destinations.If you visit a website or a page and notice mistakes you can contact the administrator and they might reward you.By providing valuable insights, students can not only earn money but also influence consumer decisions and build credibility in their chosen niche.You can also make your own websites and write blogs and earn some money


6. Resell Clothes:


Student hostels are often bustling hubs of fashion-conscious aesthetes seeking affordable and trendy clothing options. By curating a collection unique clothes you think someone can buy.You can set up a small online example or physical shop to cater to their peers' fashion needs. This side hustle allows students to combine their love for fashion with entrepreneurial skills.Gikomba is famous for having many varieties of clothes you can visit the place bargain a fair price and sell for a profit.


7. Selling Laptop Stickers and Phone Covers:


In the era of personalization, laptop stickers and phone covers have become must-have accessories for students. By creating or sourcing unique designs, students can start their own online store or participate in local markets to sell these trendy items. This side hustle not only allows for creativity but also offers a profitable business opportunity.


8.Selling instagram accounts

Creating an Instagram account, gaining followers, and subsequently selling it can be a strategic move for a student looking to explore entrepreneurial opportunities. However, it is important to approach this process ethically and responsibly.


9. Quickmart Supermarket Jobs:


For students with a flexible timetable or those comfortable working at night, supermarket jobs at< Quickmart can be an excellent choice. These jobs offer a steady income and the opportunity to work in a dynamic environment. Whether it's stocking shelves, assisting customers, or handling cash, Quickmart supermarket jobs can provide valuable work experience alongside financial stability.
