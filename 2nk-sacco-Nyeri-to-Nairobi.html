Nyeri to Nairobi Travel with 2NK Sacco: Easy Booking and Fare Information

Traveling from Nyeri to Nairobi with 2NK Sacco is a convenient and accessible option. As of 10/11/2023, the fare stands at 500 KSH, although it's essential to note that prices may change on short notice.

To secure your seat without visiting the office, simply contact the 2NK Nyeri office at 0110451341. The booking process involves calling to reserve a seat and making payment. The driver will then reach out to you with departure details.

For payments, use the Nyeri office paybill, which is 822461, with the account number specified as "2NK." However, it's advisable to call the office beforehand to confirm if there have been any changes to the paybill information.

This streamlined process ensures a smooth and hassle-free booking experience for your journey from Nyeri to Nairobi with 2NK Sacco.
