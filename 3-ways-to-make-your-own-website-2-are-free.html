If you want to make your own website,start a blog or set up a shop online I know 3 ways to archive that goal 2 ways sre completely free one way requires some finances.Some terms used will be:

  • CMS - Content Management System

    • A software that makes it easy for people to create and manage websites without needing to be tech experts.


  • Domain

    • A unique and easy-to-remember name for a website, like "example.com".


  • Subdomain

    • A smaller part of a website with its own unique address, like "subdomain.example.com".


  • Hosting

    • Renting space on a powerful computer called a server, where all the website's files are stored so that people can access the website on the internet.

Using platforms like WordPress,wix or Blogger

Wix:

  1. Sign up: Go to the Wix website (www.wix.com) and sign up for a free account. You can also sign up using your Google or Facebook account.
  2. Choose a template: Wix offers a vast selection of professionally designed templates. Choose one that suits your website's theme or niche.
  3. Customize your website: Use Wix's drag-and-drop editor to customize the template. You can add and edit elements like text, images, videos, and more. Wix also allows you to change colors, fonts, and other design aspects.
  4. Add pages: Create and organize your website's pages, such as Home, About, Contact, and any other relevant pages.
  5. Publish your website: Once you're satisfied with your website's design, click the "Publish" button to make your website live on the internet.

WordPress:

  1. Sign up: Go to WordPress.com and sign up for a free account. Choose a username, password, and a unique domain name (e.g., yoursite.wordpress.com).
  2. Choose a theme: WordPress.com offers various free themes. Select one that aligns with your website's purpose.
  3. Customize your website: Use the WordPress customizer to personalize your website's appearance. You can modify colors, headers, backgrounds, and widgets.
  4. Add content: Create and publish pages and blog posts. You can use the built-in WordPress editor, which is similar to a word processor.
  5. Go live: When you're ready to launch your website, click the "Publish" button, and your site will be live at the domain you selected during sign-up.

Blogger:

  1. Sign in: Go to www.blogger.com and sign in with your Google account. If you don't have one, you'll need to create it.
  2. Create a blog: Click on "Create New Blog." Choose a title for your blog and a domain name (e.g., yourblog.blogspot.com).
  3. Select a theme: Blogger provides several free templates. Pick a template that suits your blog's style.
  4. Customize your blog: Use the available customization options to change the blog's layout, colors, and fonts.
  5. Add content: Start writing and publishing your blog posts. You can use the simple editor provided by Blogger.
  6. Go live: When you're ready to publish your blog, click the "Publish" button, and your blog will be live on the internet.

Remember that while these platforms offer free plans, they may have some limitations, You will use the platforms subdomain untill you upgrade to a paid plan.If you're looking for more control and flexibility, consider upgrading to their premium plans or explore self-hosted options for WordPress which we will discuss next.
