If you're looking to increase your followers on Instagram, there are a few strategies you can implement.


Explore the platform: Start by exploring the Explore page on Instagram. Look for posts that are recent, probably less than a week old. Check if the users are from your local country or region.


Check the likes: Click on the likes of a post you're interested in. Follow those who have liked the post. Give high priority to those who have put stories on their pages under the "Who to Follow" section.


Be cautious: Avoid following users who don't have profile pictures or whose bio includes words like "forex" or "sell advice." Also, steer clear of accounts with many followers but low following.


Engage with stories: Check as many people's stories as possible. Users will look at how many views their story has gotten and might follow you back if they like what they see on your profile.


Repeat steps: Continue to repeat the above steps, but be cautious not to follow people consistently, as this may violate Instagram's privacy policies. Instead, participate in gain trains and unfollow people who post irrelevant content that you won't engage with.


By following these steps, you can increase your chances of gaining more followers on Instagram. Remember to engage with your followers and post relevant content to keep them interested in your profile.
