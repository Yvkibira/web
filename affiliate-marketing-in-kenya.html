Affiliate Marketing in Kenya

Affiliate marketing has emerged as a popular online business model, offering individuals the opportunity to earn passive income by promoting products or services. Kenya, with its growing e-commerce market and increasing internet penetration, presents a promising landscape for affiliate marketers. This article will provide an overview of affiliate marketing, the steps to get started, payment methods, how to become an affiliate marketer, and examples of successful affiliate programs in Kenya.


1. What exactly does an affiliate marketer do?

Affiliate marketers act as intermediaries between businesses and potential customers. Their primary role is to promote products or services through various online channels such as websites, blogs, social media platforms, or email marketing. Affiliate marketers earn a commission for each successful sale, lead, or action generated through their marketing efforts. They leverage their online presence and marketing skills to drive traffic to the merchant's website, increasing the likelihood of conversions.You might see youtube creators say their video is sponsored by some company or on Instagram their is sponsored content.


2. What is an example of an affiliate?

In Kenya, several well-known companies offer affiliate programs. Some notable examples include:


  • kilimall: Kilimall is an e-commerce platform that offers a wide range of products. Their affiliate program allows marketers to earn a commission on successful referrals to Kilimall's website.
  • Jumia: Jumia is another prominent e-commerce platform in Kenya. They offer an affiliate program that provides commissions for sales made through affiliate links.
  • Uber: Uber's affiliate program enables marketers to earn commissions by referring new drivers or riders to the platform.
  • Glovo: Glovo, a popular on-demand delivery service, offers an affiliate program for marketers to earn commissions by referring new users to the platform.
  • Safaricom: Safaricom, a leading telecommunications company in Kenya, offers an affiliate program that allows promoters to earn commissions by promoting their mobile internet bundle plan

Some Global Companies include

  • Apple: Apple's affiliate program enables marketers to earn commissions by referring customers to purchase Apple products or services.

3. How do I start affiliate marketing?

Getting started with affiliate marketing in Kenya involves a few simple steps:


  1. Choose your niche: Select a niche that aligns with your interests, knowledge, and audience's needs. This allows you to focus your efforts on a specific market segment, making it easier to attract potential customers.
  2. Research affiliate programs: Look for reputable affiliate programs that offer products or services related to your chosen niche.
  3. Build an online presence: Publish a website, blog, or social media platform to establish your online presence. Publish high-quality content that is relevant to your niche and attract readers or followers who can potentially become customers.
  4. Drive traffic: Utilize various marketing strategies such as search engine optimization (SEO), social media marketing, paid advertising, or email marketing to drive traffic to your online platforms. This increases the chances of generating conversions.
  5. Monitor and optimize: Continuously track your marketing efforts, analyze the performance of different campaigns, and optimize your strategies to maximize results.

4. How do affiliate marketers get paid?

Affiliate marketers are compensated based on predetermined commission structures. The most common payment methods include:


  • Pay-per-sale (PPS): Affiliates earn a commission when a sale is made through their referral link. The commission is usually a percentage of the sale price.
  • Pay-per-lead (PPL): Affiliates earn a commission when a potential customer completes a specific action, such as filling out a form or signing up for a newsletter.
  • Pay-per-click (PPC): Affiliates earn a commission for each click their referral link receives, regardless of whether a sale or lead is generated.

Payment is typically made on a monthly basis, with some affiliate programs having a minimum threshold that must be reached before payment is released.


5. How do I become an affiliate marketer?

To become an affiliate marketer, follow these steps:


  1. Research and choose a niche: Select a niche that interests you and has the potential for profitability.
  2. Find affiliate programs: Look for affiliate programs offered by reputable companies in your chosen niche. Research their commission structures, track record, and affiliate support.
  3. Sign up for affiliate programs: Complete the application process for the affiliate programs you've chosen. Provide accurate information and agree to the terms and conditions.
  4. Obtain affiliate links: After approval, you will receive unique affiliate links that track your referrals and ensure you receive proper credit for sales or leads generated.
  5. Promote products or services: Utilize your online platforms and marketing strategies to promote the affiliate products or services. Focus on providing value to your audience and building trust.
  6. Monitor performance and optimize: Track your campaigns, analyze performance metrics, and make adjustments to improve conversions and maximize earnings.
