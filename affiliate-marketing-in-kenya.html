Affiliate Marketing in Kenya

Affiliate marketing is a type of online advertising where individuals or companies ( affiliates) promote products or services on behalf of another business who have affiliate marketing programmes.



Affiliates (you) earn a commission for each sale or action generated through their promotional efforts. In simple terms,you sign up for a marketing program, get a unique link share it to potential customers and earn.it's like being a broker on the internet, connecting potential customers with products or services and earning a percentage of the profits for successful referrals.


How does one start affiliate marketing in Kenya

The first step is to identify companies that have affiliate marketing programmes. Sign up ,get a unique affiliate link, promote the link and earn commission on successful referrals or purchases.
