How to pay school fess for Pioneer International University via mpesa



The school fees is paid to

Consolidated bank

Paybill no..508885

Account Number 7532#admno

Steps


1.GO TO LIPA NA MPESA

2) SELECT PAY BILL

3) ENTER BUSINESS NUMBER : 508885

4) ENTER ACCOUNT NUMBER : 7532#admno (e.g. 7532#BIT/0000/2023)

5) ENTER FEES AMOUNT

6) ENTER YOUR MPESA PIN

7) CONFIRM TRANSACTION

8) RECEIVE SMS CONFIRMING THE PAYMENT IS SUCCESSFUL.

NOTE THAT YOU WILL RECEIVE TWO SMS


One from Mpesa indicating that you have sent money Consolidated bank of kenya

Another from Consolidated bank of kenya indicating that you have sent money to Pioneer international university.
