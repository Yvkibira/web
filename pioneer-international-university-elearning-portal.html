How to use Pioneer International University eLearning Portal

Pioneer International University (PIU) provides its students with an eLearning portal, offering a seamless learning experience. In this blog, we will walk you through the step-by-step process of using the PIU eLearning portal effectively.


Step 1: Accessing the Portal

To get started, open your web browser and visit the Pioneer International University website at piu.ac.ke. Once you reach the homepage, follow the subsequent steps based on the device you are using.


Step 2: Switch to Desktop Mode (Mobile Users Only)

If you are accessing the eLearning portal using a mobile device, it is recommended to switch to desktop mode for optimal viewing. Look for an option to switch to desktop mode, usually found in the browser settings. Once you enable this mode, the eLearning section will be visible at the top left corner of the page. Tap on it to proceed to the next step.



Step 3: Logging In

On the eLearning portal homepage, you will be prompted to log in. To access your courses and resources, use your PIU email account to log in. Enter your email address and password to proceed.


Step 4: Navigating the Dashboard

After logging in successfully, you will be redirected to the dashboard. On the top left corner of the dashboard, you will find your name displayed. This confirms that you are logged in. Take a moment to familiarize yourself with the layout of the dashboard.


Step 5: Exploring the Navigation Menu

On the top right corner of the dashboard, you will notice a menu button. Click on it to reveal the navigation menu. The menu provides access to different schools within PIU, including the School of Education, Information Technology, Business Management, Aerospace and Maritime, and Development and Strategic Studies. Choose the school you are enrolled in by clicking on it.



Step 6: Selecting Your Level of Study

Once you have chosen your school, you will be presented with various study levels such as degree, diploma, certificate, or professional courses. Click on the level that matches your enrollment status.


Step 7: Accessing Your Courses

After selecting your level, a list of courses available for that specific level will be displayed. Browse through the list and click on the course you wish to access.


Step 8: Enrolling in a Course

When you click on a course, you may be prompted to enter an enrollment key. This key is provided by the respective teacher or instructor. Enter the enrollment key and follow any additional instructions provided to complete the enrollment process.
