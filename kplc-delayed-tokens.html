A Guide to Obtaining Delayed KPLC Tokens

The Kenya Power and Lighting Company (KPLC) is responsible for supplying electricity to millions of Kenyan households. To access electricity, consumers typically purchase prepaid tokens that allow them to use electricity as needed. However, there are instances where delays or issues may occur in obtaining these tokens. In this article, we will provide you with a comprehensive guide on how to obtain delayed KPLC tokens and ensure

  1. Go to https://selfservice.kplc.co.ke/

  2. Tap on "Bill/Query"
  3. Select "Meter Number"

  4. Input your meter number
  5. Your last paid tokens will appear
